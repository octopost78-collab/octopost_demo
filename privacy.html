Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2024

Welcome to Social Media Manager. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our service, we collect the following information:

  • Facebook Account Information: Access to your Facebook Pages to enable posting capabilities
  • Instagram Account Information: Access to your Instagram Business account to enable posting capabilities
  • Access Tokens: OAuth access tokens to interact with Facebook and Instagram APIs on your behalf
  • Content: The posts, images, and text you choose to publish through our service

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for the following purposes:

  • To enable you to publish content to your Facebook Pages
  • To enable you to publish content to your Instagram Business accounts
  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To communicate with you about service-related matters

3. How We Store Your Information

We take data security seriously:

  • Access tokens are encrypted using industry-standard AES-256 encryption
  • All data is stored in secure, access-controlled databases
  • We use HTTPS/TLS for all data transmissions
  • Access to user data is restricted to essential personnel only

4. Information Sharing

We DO NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your information with advertisers
  • Use your content for any purpose other than providing our service
  • Access your Facebook or Instagram accounts for any unauthorized purposes

5. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our service. You can request deletion of your data at any time by following the instructions in our Data Deletion Instructions.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke access to your Facebook and Instagram accounts at any time
  • Export your data

Our service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

10. Third-Party Services
