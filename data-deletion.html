Data Deletion Instructions

We respect your right to privacy and control over your personal data. This page explains how you can request deletion of your data from our service.

⚠️ Important: Deleting your data will permanently remove all your information from our systems and cannot be undone. You will need to reconnect your accounts if you wish to use the service again.

Method 1: Revoke App Access (Immediate)

The fastest way to stop our access to your data:

  1. Go to Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites
  2. Find "Social Media Manager" in the list
  3. Click "Remove"
  4. Confirm the removal

Effect: Our access to your Facebook and Instagram accounts will be immediately revoked. Your data will be automatically deleted from our systems within 30 days.

Method 2: Email Data Deletion Request

Send an email to request complete data deletion:

  • To: privacy@socialmediamanager.com
  • Subject: Data Deletion Request
  • Include:
    • Your Facebook User ID (if known)
    • Email address associated with your account
    • Confirmation that you want all data deleted

Response Time: We will confirm receipt within 48 hours and complete deletion within 30 days.

Method 3: Automated Deletion Callback

For developers and automated systems:

Endpoint: POST https://octopost78-collab.github.io/octopost_demo/data-deletion

Parameters:

  • signed_request - Facebook signed request containing user ID

This endpoint handles data deletion requests from Facebook's Data Deletion Callback.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you request data deletion, we will permanently remove:

  • Your Facebook and Instagram access tokens
  • All content you've published through our service
  • Your account settings and preferences
  • Any logs or analytics data associated with your account
  • All personally identifiable information
Note: Content already published to your Facebook Page or Instagram account will remain on those platforms. This process only deletes data stored in our systems. To remove content from Facebook or Instagram, you must do so directly on those platforms.

Data Deletion Timeline

Our data deletion process follows this timeline:

  1. Day 0: You request data deletion
  2. Day 1-2: We confirm receipt of your request
  3. Day 3-7: Your data is marked for deletion and access is revoked
  4. Day 8-30: Complete deletion from all systems, including backups
  5. Day 30: We send confirmation that deletion is complete

Verification of Deletion

Upon completion of data deletion, we will:

  • Send a confirmation email to your registered email address
  • Provide a deletion confirmation code for your records
  • Confirm that all access tokens have been invalidated

Your Rights

Under data protection regulations (including GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Request confirmation of what personal data we hold about you
  • Request a copy of your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (as described above)
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Request data portability

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information above.
